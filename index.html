Bootcamp 2021: Spaceship Simulation

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2021). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of arrays, loops, randomness, images and animations in 2D and 3D. I created a space cockpit scene in which the camera is positioned within the cockpit and you are able to control the speed of the spaceship. The stars are arranged in a random pattern to simulate real space. As you increase the speed, the stars begin to move past you quicker and eventually you reach 'light speed' however your ship finds it difficult to withstand the pressure and a "danger" message pops up on your interface.
